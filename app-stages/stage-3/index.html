Rock, Paper, Scissors - The Classic Game!

Welcome to the Rock, Paper, Scissors game! Here are the rules:

  • You will play against the computer in a total of 5 rounds.
  • Select Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking one of the buttons.
  • The winner of each round will be displayed, and scores will be updated accordingly.
  • After each round, click "Next Round" to continue playing.
  • At the end of 5 rounds, the overall winner will be announced.
  • If you want to play again, click "Play Again" to start a new game.

Good luck, and have fun!
